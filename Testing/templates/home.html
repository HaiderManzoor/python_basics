What is SignHub?

SignHub is an open platform for the deaf and hard-of-hearing community, where users can upload and explore gesture videos. It is designed to help make communication more accessible by creating a comprehensive sign language video repository. Whether you are a learner or a teacher, SignHub aims to provide valuable resources for everyone to improve their communication skills and contribute to the development of sign language resources.
